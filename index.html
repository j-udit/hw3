how to make the perfect brownies

ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

instructions

preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine the melted butter, sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla, eggs, flour, baking powder, and salt. Spread the batter into pan. Bake in oven for 20 to 30 minutes.

Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

instructions

preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour and chocolate chips. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans. Bake in oven for 10 minutes.
